Core features

Everything you need to understand your ads

Unified Dashboard

One view across all your ad platforms. See spend, revenue, and ROAS broken down by provider, campaign, and ad set. No more jumping between tabs.

AI Copilot

Ask questions in plain English. "What's my ROAS today?" "Which campaigns performed best?" Copilot fetches live data and tells you exactly where numbers came from.

Autonomous Agents

Set rules. Let metricx act. Pause underperforming campaigns before they drain your budget. Get Slack alerts when metrics cross thresholds.

Real-Time Metrics

15-minute updates, not daily. See intraday spend progression. Catch runaway campaigns before they blow through your budget.

Verified Attribution

Shopify Web Pixel captures the full customer journey. We match UTM parameters to campaigns. See which ads drove which sales—with confidence levels.

Conversions API

We send purchase data back to Meta and Google, improving their ad optimization. Better data in means better targeting out.
